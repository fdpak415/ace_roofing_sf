It is important to check and make sure that your roof is not leaking. Roof leaks can lead to ceiling damage, attic damage, and mold issues. Please contact us for more information.

Shingle Roof

These shingles consist of a core layer made from fiberglass mat, a layer of asphalt on both the top and bottom of the shingle, and a surface coating comprised of colored mineral granules. Composite shingles are available in many colors. Their typical lifespan is around 15-30 years.

Tile Roof

Tile roofs are one of the most decorative. These tiles last a long time and require little maintenance. Clay tiles are available with a variety of sizes, colors and shapes.

Metal Roof

Compared to other roofs, metal roofs are the most durable and last a long time. Most metal roofs can be installed with a slope of 3:12 or greater. Metal roofs come in two general styles: vertical panels and interlocking shingle. We can provide you 30-50 years warranty on this style of roof.

Built-Up Roof

A waterproof membrane consisting of alternating layers of roofing fiberglass felt and hot asphalt. The typical system consists of a base sheet and two or three layers of ply sheets. A flood coat of hot asphalt is applied over the entire rooof and can be finished with a layer of clean pea gravel, a mineral-surfaced fiberglass cap sheet, or several reflective or elastometric coatings. The finish of the roof provides protection from ultraviolet sunrays, minimizes heat gain on the building, and gives the roof its required fire rating.
