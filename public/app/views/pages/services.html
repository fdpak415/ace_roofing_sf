Shingle Roof

Shingles consist of a core layer made from fiberglass mat, a layer of asphalt on both the top and bottom of the shingle, and a surface coating comprised of colored mineral granules. Composite shingles are available in many colors. Their typical lifespan is around 15-30 years.

Built-Up Roof (Bitumen)

A waterproof membrane consisting of alternating layers of roofing fiberglass felt and hot asphalt. The typical system consists of a base sheet and two or three layers of ply sheets. A flood coat of hot asphalt is applied over the entire roof and can be finished with a layer of clean pea gravel, a mineral-surfaced fiberglass cap sheet, or several reflective or elastometric coatings. The finish of the roof provides protection from ultraviolet sunrays, minimizes heat gain on the building, and gives the roof its required fire rating.

KM Coating Roof

Applying the KM Shieldcoat liquid-applied roof system to an existing roof sytem improves the roof's durability by extending the service life by 5-20 years and improving the energy efficiency of the building.

KM Shieldcoat Benefits:

  • Tear, puncture, and uplift resistant
